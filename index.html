Recipes

  1. Wash and cut 2 grams strawberries into thin chuncks
  2. Transfer into a bowl and add half a cup of sugar
  3. Squeze in half piece of lemon juice and careful with seeds
  4. Let it sit for a while to get all liquid from the fuit out
  5. Transfer into a sauce pan and place over medium to hight heat
  6. Stir continuously until thickened
  7. Turn the heat off when it is thick and allow to cool
